The Best Movies by Karla

My top 3 favorite movies

1. Sound of Freedom

This movie is based on a real life story that took place in Colombia.

I would give this movie a 8/10. A very touching movie that brings awareness.

2. The Notebook

This movie is one of the best romantic movies out there. It tells the story of how a couple went through different problems and at the end they're still together.

However, the main character in this movie gets sick and can't remember her wife.

3. The Wolf

Lastly, The Wolf is one of the best movies out there. Tells how a wolf attacks someone and the person has to survive in the middle of nowhere.

At the end, he is rescued by a woman that was just passing by and got to hear him screaming.
